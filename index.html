Who We Are

Ammon Peak was established to serve as an integrated engineering platform that seamlessly bridges field operations with cutting-edge digital technologies. We specialize in addressing critical needs within the energy sector by supplying high-performance transmission and distribution line hardware, alongside providing advanced geospatial and technological services including GIS, land surveying, 3D laser scanning, and the implementation of robust Building Information Modeling (BIM) frameworks. Our mission is to empower our partners to transform complex engineering visions into reality with precision, safety, and economic efficiency.

Global Reliability, Regional Impact

As the authorized agent of Sicame Group across 5 strategic regions (Iraq, Jordan, UAE, Egypt, Syria), Ammon Peak supplies high-performance hardware fittings for both advanced (HTLS, ACCC) and traditional conductors (ACSR, etc.). We empower the region's major power transmission projects with certified reliability and elite technical expertise.
