Supply Chain Logistics

Reliable and secure component logistics for regional infrastructure and utility-scale projects.

Seamless Cross-Border Delivery

National grid upgrades and utility lines are bound by tight construction schedules. Component supply delays can result in severe financial penalties. At Ammon Peak, we manage the complete shipping path from manufacturing plants in India to delivery locations across the Middle East (Iraq, Jordan, UAE, Egypt, and Syria).

We handle customs clearance, import documentation, and localized compliance standards, guaranteeing that structural accessories arrive securely at local grid locations without delays.

Compliance Management

Ensuring all imported components strictly comply with certificate of origin rules, custom taxes, and local utility regulations.

Fast-Track Delivery

Direct freight lines and local transit paths mapped to expedite the movement of heavy steel and composite components.

Warehousing Support

Staging grid components and specialized installation tooling in regional hubs to match construction flow schedules.
