Building Information Modeling (BIM)

Building Information Modeling (BIM) & MEP Design

We are a multidisciplinary engineering consultancy specializing in Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing (MEP) Engineering Design and Building Information Modeling (BIM), delivering integrated engineering solutions for residential, commercial, hospitality, healthcare, industrial, educational, mixed-use, and infrastructure developments.

Our approach combines engineering excellence, innovation, and practical design to deliver high-performance systems that are efficient, sustainable, constructible, and tailored to each project's unique objectives. From concept design through detailed design, tender documentation, construction support, and project close-out, we provide complete engineering solutions throughout the entire project lifecycle.

MEP Engineering Design

Our engineering services include HVAC, Plumbing, Drainage, Fire Fighting, Fire Alarm Coordination, Medical Gas Systems, Electrical Power, Lighting, ELV Systems, Infrastructure Utilities, and multidisciplinary coordination. We prepare comprehensive engineering deliverables including Design Drawings, Shop Drawings, As-Built Drawings, Engineering Calculations, Technical Specifications, BOQs, Design Reports, and Construction Documentation.

BIM Methodologies (LOD 100–500)

Through advanced BIM methodologies, we develop intelligent digital models across all Levels of Development (LOD 100–500), enabling multidisciplinary coordination, clash detection, constructability reviews, quantity take-offs, digital documentation, and seamless collaboration between designers, contractors, and project stakeholders throughout design, construction, and facility management.

Compliance & Regulations

Compliance is at the core of our engineering philosophy. Every project is developed in accordance with the latest international engineering standards and local authority regulations, ensuring full compliance with project-specific statutory requirements. Our designs are produced to satisfy the requirements of Authorities Having Jurisdiction (AHJs), Civil Defense and Fire & Life Safety (FLS) regulations, applicable international codes and standards, and client-specific design criteria.

Sustainability Principles

We integrate sustainability principles into every stage of the design process by considering energy efficiency, water conservation, indoor environmental quality, and long-term operational performance. Our engineering solutions support sustainable building strategies and are developed to meet LEED requirements and other project-specific sustainability objectives whenever applicable.

Value Engineering

Value Engineering is an essential part of our design process. We continuously evaluate alternative engineering solutions to optimize capital and operational costs without compromising safety, functionality, quality, or regulatory compliance. Through careful system selection, coordination, and lifecycle cost analysis, we help clients maximize project value while improving constructability and long-term building performance.

Software & Tools

Utilizing industry-leading software including Autodesk Revit, Navisworks, AutoCAD, HAP, and other engineering analysis platforms, we deliver coordinated, accurate, and construction-ready engineering solutions that reduce risk, minimize rework, and enhance project efficiency.

"Our commitment is to provide technically outstanding engineering services built on quality, precision, integrity, innovation, and client satisfaction, creating engineering solutions that meet today's requirements while preparing projects for the future."
